# 🧪 Chemistry Lab Simulator
> An immersive Virtual Reality application for laboratory safety training built for Meta Quest 2/3.

---

## 💡 Project Idea

Chemistry Lab Simulator is a VR training application that teaches laboratory safety procedures through hands-on immersive scenarios. Instead of reading safety manuals or watching videos, users physically interact with lab equipment in a risk-free virtual environment — making training more engaging, memorable, and effective.

---

## 🎯 Problem We're Solving

Traditional lab safety training is:
- **Passive** — reading manuals or watching videos
- **Expensive** — requires physical equipment and consumables
- **Risky** — mistakes during training can cause real harm
- **Inconsistent** — quality varies depending on the trainer

Our VR simulator makes training **interactive, repeatable, cost-effective and completely safe.**

---

## 🔬 Planned Training Scenarios

### Scenario 1 — PPE Training
Players must correctly identify and wear Personal Protective Equipment before entering the lab — safety goggles, lab coat, and gloves.

### Scenario 2 — Fire Extinguisher
Players face an active lab fire and must pick up a fire extinguisher and extinguish it correctly.

### Scenario 3 — Chemical Hazard Identification
Players sort chemical bottles into the correct storage cabinets based on their hazard type — flammable, corrosive, or general.

### Scenario 4 — Chemical Mixing Experiment
Players pick up labeled reagent bottles and pour them into a mixing beaker. The system shows whether the combination is safe or dangerous — with a real scientific explanation of why.
